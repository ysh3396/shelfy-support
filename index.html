About

Shelfy turns any link into organized knowledge. Share a YouTube video, LinkedIn post, or web article — Shelfy automatically reads it and creates AI summaries so you never lose an insight.

Features

  • Instant AI Summaries — Key Points, Detailed, Easy Explain, Background, Q&A
  • Share Sheet Integration — Save links from any app
  • Smart Collections — Organize content into folders
  • Push Notifications — Get notified when summaries are ready
  • Multi-language — English, Korean, Japanese

Privacy Policy

Shelfy collects minimal data required to provide the service:

  • Account information (email) for authentication
  • URLs you save for content processing
  • Push notification tokens for delivery

We do not sell your data to third parties. Content you save is stored securely and only accessible to you.
